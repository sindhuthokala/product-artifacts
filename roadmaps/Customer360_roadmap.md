# Customer360 Intelligent Insights Platform – Roadmap (Phase 0–2)

## Phase 0 – Discovery & Alignment (Weeks 1–2)
- Confirm objectives and success metrics  
- Identify core source systems (CRM, web, transactions)  
- Align with data engineering, analytics, and marketing teams  
- Finalize initial scope and constraints  

## Phase 1 – Data Foundation (Weeks 3–8)
- Design core data model for unified profiles  
- Implement ingestion from 3 core systems  
- Configure identity resolution rules  
- Stand up Customer360 analytics views/tables  
- Deliver initial retention/engagement dashboard  

## Phase 2 – Insights & Activation (Weeks 9–14)
- Define initial high-value segments (e.g., at-risk, high LTV)  
- Enable export/activation of segments to one downstream channel  
- Prototype churn-risk scoring or simple prediction model  
- Refine dashboards using stakeholder feedback  

## Phase 3 – Optimization & Expansion (Future)
- Introduce additional data sources (support, product usage)  
- Expand activation channels (ads, journeys, messaging)  
- Support advanced AI-based recommendations  
- Strengthen monitoring, quality checks, and governance
